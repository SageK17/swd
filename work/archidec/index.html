Our Founder

Meet Archi Acheampong

Archi Acheampong is the Director of Archi & Dec Gh Co. Ltd, the official West African branch of DEC International B.V. Operating under the leadership of DEC International CEO T.A. de Goeij, Archi manages all West African operations including product distribution, AC installation, and technical support throughout the region.

The company name tells the story — combining "Archi" (the founder's name) with "Dec" (DEC International), symbolizing the bridge between Dutch engineering excellence and Ghanaian entrepreneurship.

Based in Accra, Archi leads a team dedicated to world-class central AC installation, flexible duct supply, and ongoing technical support tailored to Ghana's tropical climate.

Worldwide Project References

DEC International products are installed in iconic buildings across 50+ countries.

Project Portfolio

Iconic Projects Powered by DEC

From world-renowned airports to historic palaces — discover how DEC ducting solutions serve the most demanding HVAC applications worldwide.

Beijing, China

Beijing Capital International Airport

Located 32km northeast of Beijing's city center, this is one of the world's busiest airports. DEC International supplied flexible ducting throughout the massive terminal complex — one of the largest airport HVAC projects ever undertaken, demonstrating DEC's capacity to deliver on the grandest scale.

St. Petersburg, Russia

Winter Palace / Hermitage Museum

The former official residence of Russian monarchs (1732–1917), now housing the world-famous Hermitage Museum. DEC flexible ducts were chosen for their low-profile installation capability in historic spaces, providing precise climate control essential for preserving priceless artworks and artefacts.

Poznań, Poland

Volkswagen Production Facility

A joint venture between Volkswagen AG and Polish manufacturer Tarpan. DEC International supplied comprehensive ducting solutions for the demanding industrial HVAC requirements of automotive manufacturing, where precise environmental control is critical for production quality.

Paris, France

La Défense Business District

Europe's largest purpose-built business district covering 560 hectares with 72 glass and steel buildings, 180,000 daily workers, and 3,500,000 m² of office space. DEC ducting products serve the climate systems throughout this iconic Parisian commercial hub.

Parnitha, Greece

Regency Casino Mont Parnes

Located at 1,055 meters altitude in the heart of Parnitha National Forest, this resort overlooks the entire Attica basin to the sea. DEC International ducting provides climate control in this challenging high-altitude mountain environment with extreme temperature variations.

London, United Kingdom

Emirates Stadium

E and S Installation fitted the ventilation system of this 60,000+ seat football stadium, choosing DEC International because the SonoDEC® series has exceptional sound attenuation properties and the flexible ducts meet the highest quality standards for spectator comfort.

Additional References

More Notable Projects

Tokuda Hospital, Sofia

DEC International was selected for its innovative character, high quality, and versatile assortment. This durable choice ensures top-class patient comfort. The hospital received the "Building of the Year" award.

München Airport

ABB Gebäudetechnik AG chose DEC International for its global character, high quality work, certified products and versatile assortment. DEC also stands for durability and safety in airport environments.

Hong Kong Shanghai Bank HQ

DEC supplied tailor-made flexible ducts in special diameters meeting technical air requirements for rigid ductwork. All ducts use a SafeDEC® male/female connection with rubber seal for airtight assembly.

WTC Ljubljana

State-of-the-art comfort and noise requirements met with SonoDEC® noise-reducing flexible air duct systems. The WTC Ljubljana was among the first 15 WTCs awarded the WTCA certificate.

Hilton Athens

Hilton stands for exclusivity, high comfort, luxury and top quality. Given DEC's high quality and number of international certificates, brand preference went to DEC International for the complete HVAC ducting solution.

Westhafen Tower, Frankfurt

The only skyscraper on the water — Frankfurt's best-known Main river landmark. DEC International was chosen for its global character, high quality, product certificates and assortment versatility.
