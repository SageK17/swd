The Story — 001

One cell changed everything.

Founder Hector Jimenez was born with sickle cell anemia. An inheritance, as he puts it — one that could have easily broken his spirit. Instead, it became the source of his strength.

That is why the brand carries the name. The Sickled Cell is about taking a negative and making it a positive — about never giving up on your dreams or your goals. Every piece is made from scratch, not a logo slapped on a blank, because a message this personal deserves garments built to last.

It stays in the family, too. The Sickled Cell is a family-owned company, and a percentage of profits goes toward events that bring sickle cell awareness — including a day of cultural arts.

"It's not just a brand. It's a way of life."

— Hector Jimenez, founder
